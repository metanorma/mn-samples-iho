<clause id="_safety_of_navigation_standards" obligation="normative">
<title>Safety of Navigation Standards</title>
<p id="_56c5b939-e275-1294-dd18-0ab89ded8bcf">Minimum bathymetry standards are defined in <xref target="tab-minimum-bathymetry"/>. Other minimum standards for positioning and water flow measurements are defined in <xref target="tab-other-minimum"/>. Both tables must be read in conjunction with the detailed text in this document.</p>

<p id="_8931847b-025e-a8dd-75ab-83fb74a2f57e">As stated above, all standards defined in <xref target="tab-minimum-bathymetry"/> and <xref target="tab-other-minimum"/> are included in the specification <xref target="scl-matrix-description">Matrix</xref> within ranges of specification values which are available to enhance and customise safety of navigation surveys. Although the <xref target="scl-matrix-description">Matrix</xref> is available for this purpose, its usage will not reduce the minimum standards defined for safety of navigation survey orders. See Annex A for guidance on how to use the Specification <xref target="scl-matrix-description">Matrix</xref>.</p>
