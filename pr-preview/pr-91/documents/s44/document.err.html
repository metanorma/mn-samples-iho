<title id="_dc51df2f-4d2a-d20e-488d-eed17fe1e327">Bathymetric Coverage</title>
<p id="_bf8579fc-c141-e18e-5ea0-79b204fd6907">The concept of <xref target="def-bathymetric_coverage"> <display-text>bathymetric coverage</display-text> </xref> was introduced in this edition of S-44, in order to make the standard technology independent. Acquisition of <xref target="def-bathymetric_coverage"> <display-text>bathymetric coverage</display-text> </xref> requires use of a sensor that measures and records depths. <xref target="tab-minimum-bathymetry"/> specifies the minimum <xref target="def-bathymetric_coverage"> <display-text>bathymetric coverage</display-text> </xref> to be achieved by each survey order.</p>

<p id="_137a096f-f081-ed48-4ae7-f11e645e59cb">Minimum bathymetry standards are defined in <xref target="tab-minimum-bathymetry"/>. Other minimum standards for positioning and water flow measurements are defined in <xref target="tab-other-minimum"/>. Both tables must be read in conjunction with the detailed text in this document.</p>

<p id="_bf971136-f6c2-2ada-fcff-287909f7c565">As stated above, all standards defined in <xref target="tab-minimum-bathymetry"/> and <xref target="tab-other-minimum"/> are included in the specification <xref target="scl-matrix-description"> <display-text>Matrix</display-text> </xref> within ranges of specification values which are available to enhance and customise safety of navigation surveys. Although the <xref target="scl-matrix-description"> <display-text>Matrix</display-text> </xref> is available for this purpose, its usage will not reduce the minimum standards defined for safety of navigation survey orders. See Annex A for guidance on how to use the Specification <xref target="scl-matrix-description"> <display-text>Matrix</display-text> </xref>.</p>
